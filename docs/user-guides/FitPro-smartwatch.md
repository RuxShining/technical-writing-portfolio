## Getting Started

### Initial Setup

1. **Charge your watch**
   - Connect the magnetic charging cable to the back of the watch
   - Plug the USB end into a power adapter or computer
   - Charge for at least 2 hours before first use
   - The screen will display the battery percentage

2. **Power on the device**
   - Press and hold the side button for 3 seconds
   - Select your language from the on-screen menu
   - Swipe through the welcome screens

3. **Download the FitPro app**
   - Available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play)
   - Search for "FitPro Connect"
   - Download and install the app on your smartphone

4. **Pair with your smartphone**
   - Open the FitPro Connect app
   - Create an account or log in
   - Tap "Add Device" and select "FitPro X5"
   - Follow the on-screen pairing instructions
   - Grant necessary permissions (Bluetooth, notifications, location)

### Initial Configuration

After pairing, the app will prompt you to enter:

- Personal information (age, height, weight, gender)
- Fitness goals (daily steps, active minutes, calorie targets)
- Notification preferences
- Display settings

**Tip:** Accurate personal information ensures more precise calorie and fitness calculations.

## Fitness Tracking Features

### Step Counter

Your watch automatically counts your steps throughout the day.

**To view step count:**
1. From the home screen, swipe left to the Steps widget
2. View daily steps, distance, and calories burned
3. Check progress toward your daily goal

**Tips for accurate tracking:**
- Wear the watch snugly on your wrist
- Ensure the watch is positioned 1-2 finger widths above your wrist bone
- Calibrate stride length in the app settings for better distance accuracy

### Workout Modes

The FitPro X5 supports 15+ workout modes:

- Running (outdoor/indoor)
- Walking
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Yoga
- Strength training
- HIIT
- Elliptical
- Rowing
- And more...

**Starting a workout:**
1. Press the side button to access the main menu
2. Tap "Workout"
3. Select your activity type
4. Tap the play icon to begin
5. The watch will start GPS tracking (for outdoor activities)

**During workout:**
- View real-time stats: time, heart rate, calories, distance
- Swipe right to pause or end the workout
- Auto-pause feature activates when you stop moving

**After workout:**
- Review workout summary on the watch
- Detailed analysis available in the FitPro Connect app
- View route maps for GPS-tracked activities

### Auto-Detection

The watch can automatically detect and track certain activities:

- Walking (after 10 minutes of continuous movement)
- Running (after 5 minutes)
- Cycling (after 10 minutes)

Enable this feature in: **App > Device Settings > Auto-Detect Workouts**

## Health Monitoring

### Heart Rate Monitoring

**Continuous monitoring:**
- Enable 24/7 heart rate tracking in the app
- The watch measures your heart rate every 5 minutes
- Alerts you if resting heart rate is abnormally high or low

**Manual measurement:**
1. Navigate to "Heart Rate" widget
2. Stay still and wait 30 seconds
3. View current heart rate and resting heart rate data

**During workouts:**
- Real-time heart rate displayed on screen
- Heart rate zones help optimize training intensity

### Sleep Tracking

The watch automatically monitors your sleep when worn at night.

**Sleep metrics tracked:**
- Total sleep time
- Sleep stages (light, deep, REM)
- Sleep score (0-100)
- Wake-up times

**Viewing sleep data:**
- Check the Sleep widget on your watch
- Detailed analysis in the FitPro Connect app
- Weekly and monthly sleep trends

**Tips for better tracking:**
- Wear the watch comfortably (not too tight)
- Enable sleep mode to reduce nighttime disruptions
- Set sleep goals in the app

### Blood Oxygen (SpO2) Monitoring

Measure your blood oxygen saturation levels.

**To measure SpO2:**
1. Navigate to the SpO2 widget
2. Keep your wrist steady and at heart level
3. Wait 30-45 seconds for the reading
4. Normal range: 95-100%

**Note:** This feature is for general wellness purposes only and not intended for medical diagnosis.

### Stress Monitoring

The watch uses heart rate variability (HRV) to estimate stress levels.

**Stress levels:**
- Relaxed (1-25)
- Normal (26-50)
- Medium (51-75)
- High (76-100)

**Breathing exercises:**
When stress is detected, the watch suggests guided breathing sessions to help you relax.

## Battery & Charging

### Battery Life

- **Typical use:** 7-10 days
- **Heavy use** (GPS workouts daily): 5-7 days
- **Always-on display enabled:** 3-5 days

### Charging Instructions

1. Clean the charging contacts on the watch back
2. Attach magnetic charging cable (it snaps into place)
3. Connect USB to power source
4. Charging indicator appears on screen
5. Full charge takes approximately 2 hours

**Charging tips:**
- Avoid charging in extreme temperatures
- Don't use damaged charging cables
- Battery life may decrease over time (normal battery aging)

### Battery Saving Tips

- Reduce screen brightness
- Shorten screen timeout duration
- Disable always-on display
- Turn off continuous heart rate monitoring
- Limit GPS workout frequency
- Disable unused smart features

### Low Battery Alerts

- 20% battery: First notification
- 10% battery: Second notification
- 5% battery: Final warning, watch enters power-saving mode

## Troubleshooting

### Watch won't turn on

**Solutions:**
- Charge the watch for at least 30 minutes
- Try a different USB power adapter
- Press and hold the side button for 10 seconds to force restart

### Not syncing with smartphone

**Solutions:**
- Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
- Check that FitPro Connect app is running
- Restart both watch and smartphone
- Re-pair the device in the app
- Update to the latest firmware version

### Inaccurate step count

**Solutions:**
- Wear watch snugly on wrist
- Position watch correctly (1-2 fingers above wrist bone)
- Calibrate stride length in app settings
- Update firmware to latest version

### GPS not working

**Solutions:**
- Ensure location permissions are granted
- Start workout outdoors with clear sky view
- Wait 1-2 minutes for GPS signal acquisition
- Check that GPS is enabled for the activity
- Update watch firmware

### Heart rate not reading

**Solutions:**
- Ensure watch is worn snugly
- Clean the sensors on the watch back
- Remove wrist hair that may interfere
- Warm up before measurement (cold skin affects accuracy)
- Stay still during measurement

### Battery draining quickly

**Solutions:**
- Check battery usage in Settings > Battery
- Disable unused features
- Reduce screen brightness
- Shorten screen timeout
- Turn off always-on display
- Update to latest firmware

### Screen not responding

**Solutions:**
- Clean the screen
- Remove screen protector if installed
- Dry the screen and your fingers
- Restart the watch
- Factory reset if problem persists

### Factory Reset

**Warning:** This will erase all data on your watch.

**To reset:**
1. Settings > System > Factory Reset
2. Or: Long press side button > Power Off > press side button 5 times rapidly during power-off animation

**After reset:**
- Re-pair watch with FitPro Connect app
- Reconfigure settings and preferences

### Water Resistance Guidelines

**5ATM (50m) rating means the watch can withstand:**
- Washing hands and face
- Light rain
- Swimming in pools and open water
- Snorkeling

**NOT suitable for:**
- Diving
- High-pressure water activities
- Hot showers or saunas (steam damages seals)
- Water skiing or high-speed water sports

**After water exposure:**
- Rinse with fresh water if exposed to chlorine or salt water
- Dry thoroughly before charging
- Check that charging port is dry

## Safety Information

### General Safety

- Not intended for medical diagnosis or treatment
- Consult a doctor before starting any new exercise program
- If you experience discomfort while wearing the watch, remove it immediately
- Keep watch and accessories away from children under 3 years (choking hazard)

### Skin Irritation Prevention

- Clean the watch and wrist regularly
- Don't wear too tightly

### Electrical Safety

- Use only the provided charging cable
- Don't charge near water or flammable materials
- Unplug during lightning storms
- Don't attempt to disassemble or repair the watch yourself

## Glossary

**5ATM:** Water resistance rating (50 meters)

**BPM:** Beats per minute (heart rate measurement)

**GPS:** Global Positioning System for tracking outdoor activities

**HRV:** Heart Rate Variability (used for stress measurement)

**REM:** Rapid Eye Movement (sleep stage)

**SpO2:** Blood oxygen saturation level

**VO2 Max:** Maximum oxygen consumption during exercise (advanced fitness metric)
